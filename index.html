Cinco De Mayo

This App generates recipes and beverages in preparation for Cinco De Mayo. First feature is the countdown till Cinco De Mayo (May 5, 2021). The App offers the possibility of generating a random recipe for a large variety of tacos or a random beverage and its ingredients. The App procceeds to two taco categories; displaying meat tacos, and then vegetarian. These options will be chosen by recipe name and once selected, the results are displayed at the bottom of the page. The Apps final function offers the option of searching through our choices of beverages. To do so you can eather type a keyword or choose a common ingredient from our dropdown list. Once the user has selected or entered an option, the beverage name, picture, instructions and ingredients are displayed. To organize the app you can close all results searched to return to the default display of the web page. Local storage saves the user's searches. Our algorithm displays a notification if no results are found for a given search.
